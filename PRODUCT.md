# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Operations leads, SREs, QA leads, and compliance managers who need to standardize tribal knowledge into formal procedures. They are often busy, multitasking, and need high confidence that the AI-generated output is accurate and complete before distributing it to their teams.

## Product Purpose
Stepease automates the creation and maintenance of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). It transforms conversational knowledge into structured documentation, reducing the time spent on writing from weeks to minutes while ensuring compliance and consistency.

## Brand Personality
A friendly expert assistant who makes complex things feel simple. The voice is confident, efficient, and direct, but approachable. It guides users without being patronizing.

## Anti-references
- **The "AI Slop" look:** Avoid unnecessary glowing gradients, purple/pink neon overlays, and excessive glassmorphism that screams "AI wrapper."
- **Boring Government Portals:** Avoid flat, monolithic forms, overwhelming dense text, and a lack of visual hierarchy that makes the process feel bureaucratic.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Cleverness:** Prioritize legibility and information hierarchy. The user should always know what is "Review," "Note," and "Action."
- **Expert Confidence:** The UI should feel robust and reliable. Every interaction should reinforce that the system has captured the details correctly.
- **Surgical Precision:** Edits and revisions should feel precise. Show only what matters.
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Don't overwhelm the user. Show high-level statuses (e.g., v1, v2) and allow drilling down into details (e.g., revision history).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- Support for keyboard navigation in all modals and data tables.
- Clear focus indicators and high contrast for text-heavy SOP content.
